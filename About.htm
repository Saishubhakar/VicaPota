Chiranjeevi Veerabhadra Raju
C. Veerabhadra Raju (Dec 1945 - Nov 2017), a dedicated Indian Army soldier and the first-generation technocrat of the Chiranjeevi Group. His life was a celebration of service to the nation, blending technical expertise with a profound commitment to defending our country. After his military service, he transitioned into successful ventures such as transport, dairy, and estates, showcasing visionary leadership and unwavering dedication. A defense technocrat and service professional, C. Veerabahdra Raju exemplified patriotism through his noteworthy contributions. His legacy lives on, and we continue to honor and celebrate the spirit of his patriotic work in every aspect of our lives. His entrepreneurial spirit and resilience have become the foundation of the Chiranjeevi Group, leaving an indelible mark on both business and service to the nation.
