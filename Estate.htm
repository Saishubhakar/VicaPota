ESTATE

Real estate refers to property consisting of land and the buildings on it, along with its natural resources such as crops, minerals, or water. It encompasses residential, commercial, and industrial properties, as well as vacant land. The real estate market involves the buying, selling, and leasing of these properties, driven by factors such as location, economic conditions, and market demand. Real estate is a significant component of wealth for individuals and a key driver of economic activity, influencing both local and global economies. It plays a crucial role in shaping communities, providing homes and spaces for businesses, and serving as an investment asset class.
