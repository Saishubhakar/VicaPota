SOLAR GROSS & NET-METERING   (ON - GRID)
Solar panel array facing the sun captures the sun energy, converts it into electricity to give uninterrupted power supply. With the help of inverter, the DC power generated from the solar array is converted to AC power. The loads operate as per the requirement of the end user and surplus, if any is fed to the grid.That means when your solar panels produce electricity, it goes first to powering the lights, appliances and electronics in your office/ home. If your solar power system produces more electricity than you need, the extra electricity gets delivered back to the utility grid. If your solar power system produces less electricity on cloudy days, the required power is automatically supplied from the utility grid.
SOLAR POWER SYSTEM (OFF GRID)
SOLAR POWER SYSTEMS (Solar power Systems off grid) Solar power systems are designed for use in any place, under whatever the situation where good quality power is needed. Specially, these have the potential to provide uninterrupted power for all areas where security and safety are priorities. Solar power packs work on their own, independent of conventional power supply.
BOOT MODEL
 we operate under OPEX / BOOT model (Build, Own, Operate and Transfer) and deliver a 100% integrated Solar Power as a Service (SPAAS) detailed below:
1)     We set up on-site solar power plants (on rooftops, as carports, on grounds and/or on superstructures). These plants integrate seamlessly with the customer’s existing grid and/or DG system.

2)     The solar plants are set up entirely on our own cost and are also maintained by us throughout the project term.

3)     We enter into a long term (15-25 years) Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) with our customers. At the end of the PPA term, the plant is transferred free of cost (usually INR 1) to the customer.

4)     The electricity price for solar power is agreed for the entire PPA period at the beginning of the agreement. This price is always lower than the existing grid tariff, ensuring guaranteed and continuous savings to the customer throughout the term of the PPA.

5)     We do the end-to-end delivery of the plant from designing, procurement, installation, commissioning and operation & Maintenance.

6)     We ensure that our solar plant has a 99%+ Up-time.

7)     We bill our customers every month on the basis of actual energy generated by the solar plant. This ensures that the entire performance risk is borne by us as we only get paid for what we generate.

8)     We undertake stiff plant performance targets and agree to compensate the customer if such targets are not met.
